# Legal Notice & Content Policy

## What ComicArc Is

ComicArc is a **local, personal media organizer** for comic book files. It runs entirely on your own computer, serves content only to you, and has no ability to share, distribute, upload, or transmit files to any third party. No comic content is ever sent to an external server.

This is the same model as:
- **iTunes / Apple Music** — organizes and plays music files on your device
- **Plex / Infuse / VLC** — organizes and plays video files on your device
- **Calibre** — organizes and reads ebook files on your device

ComicArc organizes and reads comic files on your device. That is the full extent of what it does.

---

## Intended Use — Personal Files You Own

ComicArc is designed for use with comic files that you have a legal right to possess and read. Examples of legitimate use include:

- **Digital purchases you have downloaded** — comics bought from platforms like ComiXology, Marvel, DC, or Image that allow file download
- **Physical media you own** — if you own a physical comic and have created a digital backup for personal use
- **DRM-free files** — comics distributed without copy protection by the publisher or creator
- **Your own original work** — files you have created yourself
- **Public domain comics** — works where copyright has expired (pre-1928 U.S. publications, or works explicitly released to the public domain)
- **Creative Commons licensed works** — comics distributed under a license that permits personal use

## What ComicArc Is NOT For

ComicArc must not be used to access, organize, store, or read:

- Comics downloaded without authorization from the copyright holder
- Comics obtained from piracy sites, torrent networks, or unauthorized sources
- Files that were ripped from subscription services (Marvel Unlimited, DC Universe Infinite, ComiXology Unlimited, etc.) in violation of those services' terms of use
- Any content you do not have the legal right to possess in the jurisdiction where you live

## No Distribution

ComicArc has no sharing, upload, or publishing functionality. It cannot:

- Send your files to other users
- Make your library accessible to the internet

## DRM Notice

ComicArc does not circumvent, bypass, or interact with Digital Rights Management (DRM) systems. It reads standard, unprotected file formats (CBZ, CBR, PDF, common image formats). Circumventing DRM — even for personal backup purposes — may be illegal under laws such as the U.S. Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) or equivalent laws in other countries. ComicArc does not assist with, enable, or encourage DRM circumvention.

## A Note on "Personal Use" and Format-Shifting

Some jurisdictions recognize a limited right to make personal copies of media you legally own, commonly called "format-shifting" (e.g., ripping a CD you bought to MP3 for your own listening). Whether this doctrine applies to comic books, and to what extent, varies by country and has not been definitively settled in most jurisdictions.

**This software does not constitute legal advice.** If you are unsure whether a specific use is legal in your jurisdiction, consult a qualified attorney.
